# Pre-Startup Safety Inspection Checklist

**Document No.:** ZJP-SOP-007  
**Revision:** 3.0  
**Effective Date:** 2021-12-01  
**Applicability:** Unit 1 — Required before EVERY boiler startup (cold, hot, or post-maintenance)

> **This checklist must be completed, signed, and retained for 12 months. Startup must not proceed until all items are checked and any open items dispositioned.**

---

## Part A: Documentation and Permits

- [ ] All active work orders against the unit are closed or formally deferred
- [ ] Isolation register reviewed — all LOTO devices accounted for and removed
- [ ] Last shift log entry reviewed — no outstanding unresolved issues
- [ ] Previous startup inspection checklist reviewed — any carry-over items addressed

---

## Part B: Furnace and Refractory

- [ ] All furnace access doors, manholes, and inspection ports closed and bolted (verify torque if post-maintenance)
- [ ] Furnace expansion joints visually inspected from exterior — no visible gaps or damage
- [ ] Bottom ash system cleared — no blockages in bottom hoppers
- [ ] Furnace interior cleared if any personnel access was performed (confined space entry log reviewed)

---

## Part C: Rotating Equipment Pre-Start Checks

### IDF
- [ ] Oil level in bearing housings within sight glass range
- [ ] Coupling guard installed and secure
- [ ] Inlet damper confirmed CLOSED (pre-start position)
- [ ] No tools, rags, or foreign objects on fan or in ductwork
- [ ] Jog test performed (2-second run) — correct rotation confirmed, no metallic sounds
- [ ] YFJ3_ZD1 and YFJ3_ZD2 transmitters reading (will show near-zero when stopped — confirm live signal)

### Primary Fan
- [ ] Oil level confirmed
- [ ] Coupling guard secure
- [ ] Outlet damper confirmed CLOSED
- [ ] Correct rotation confirmed (arrow on casing matches jog direction)

### Secondary Fan
- [ ] Same checks as primary fan

---

## Part D: Steam Side and Pressure Parts

- [ ] Steam drum water level visible in gauge glass at correct cold level
- [ ] All drum drain valves closed (except vents)
- [ ] Drum air vent valves open (unit depressurized) or positioned per startup procedure
- [ ] Superheater drain valves cracked open for warm-up drainage
- [ ] Main steam isolation valve: CLOSED
- [ ] Safety valve test lever: do NOT test (test performed separately per maintenance schedule)
- [ ] No active steam or water leaks visible from last outage repair work

---

## Part E: Instrumentation

- [ ] All DCS analog inputs showing plausible readings (no flat-line or clearly failed sensors)
- [ ] O2 analyzers (AIR_8301A, AIR_8301B) powered on, calibration within 7-day interval
- [ ] Furnace pressure transmitters (PT_8313A–F): all reading near zero Pa (atmospheric)
- [ ] BPS (boiler protection system) functional test completed within last 30 days (review test log)
- [ ] All critical alarm setpoints correct per ZJP-CTRL-004 (Alarm Setpoint Register)

---

## Part F: Fuel System

- [ ] Coal feeder gates: CLOSED, ready for controlled opening during startup
- [ ] Coal bunker level: ≥ 30% (confirm sufficient coal for startup sequence)
- [ ] Ignition fuel (light oil) system: fuel supply valve open, day tank level adequate
- [ ] Ignition burner valves: in closed/ready position
- [ ] Coal pipeline purge air: available and pressurized

---

## Part G: Environmental and Safety

- [ ] Ash removal system ready — bottom ash cooler and fly ash conveying available
- [ ] All personnel not involved in startup have cleared the boiler area
- [ ] Spotter confirmed at control room to monitor DCS during startup
- [ ] Emergency shower/eyewash in boiler house tested (weekly test log current)
- [ ] Fire extinguishers in place and current inspection tag
